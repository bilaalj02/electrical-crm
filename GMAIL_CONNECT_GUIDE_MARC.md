# How to Connect Your Gmail to MES CRM
**For Marc**
**Estimated time: 2 minutes**

Once you're done, your business emails will appear inside the CRM, you can compose and send from there, and emails about new jobs will be auto-flagged for you.

---

## Step 1 — Log in to your CRM

Open **https://meselectrical-crm.vercel.app** and sign in with your account.

> [SCREENSHOT 1 — CRM login screen with your credentials filled in]

---

## Step 2 — Go to the Emails section

In the sidebar on the left, click the **Emails** icon (envelope).

> [SCREENSHOT 2 — Emails section showing "Connected Accounts" with "+ Connect Account" button highlighted in gold]

---

## Step 3 — Click "Connect Account" and choose Gmail

A small window will pop up with two options — Gmail and Microsoft. Click **Gmail**.

> [SCREENSHOT 3 — The "Connect Email Account" modal showing Gmail and Microsoft options, with Gmail highlighted]

---

## Step 4 — Sign in with your Gmail and approve

A Google sign-in window appears. Sign in with your business Gmail address.

**Important — you may see this warning from Google:**

> *"Google hasn't verified this app. The app is requesting access to sensitive info in your Google Account."*

This is normal. Our app is new and going through Google's review process. As long as you trust us (you do — this is your CRM), do the following:

1. Click **Advanced** at the bottom-left of the warning
2. Click **Go to MES Electrical CRM (unsafe)**
3. On the next screen, review the permissions and click **Allow**

> [SCREENSHOT 4 — Google "unverified app" warning with "Advanced" button highlighted, then the consent screen with "Allow" highlighted]

---

## Step 5 — You're connected

You'll be brought back to your CRM. Your Gmail account is now listed under **Connected Accounts**, and your emails will start appearing within about 60 seconds.

> [SCREENSHOT 5 — Emails section now showing your Gmail address under Connected Accounts, with recent emails listed below]

---

## What happens now

- Your inbox refreshes automatically every 15 minutes
- You can compose and send emails directly from the CRM — they'll send from your Gmail address
- The system will flag emails that look like new job leads (you'll see them highlighted in the inbox)
- All emails are private to you — they don't appear in any office staff member's account

---

## To disconnect later

If you ever want to disconnect your Gmail:
1. Emails section → **Connected Accounts**
2. Click the trash icon next to your address

The CRM will stop syncing. Your past emails inside the CRM stay; new ones won't come in until you reconnect.

---

## If something goes wrong

Send Elvis a screenshot of whatever error you see and he'll sort it. The most common one is "Access blocked" — that just means your email needs to be added to a test list on our side, which takes 30 seconds.
